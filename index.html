The Intense Side

Discover my discipline, workout routine, and hardcore side of life.

Fitness Regimen

My workout plan follows a dedicated Push Pull Legs split, training 6 days a week with intensity and focus. This structured approach has helped me develop both physical strength and mental discipline that carries over into other aspects of my life. Beyond the physical benefits, working out serves as a crucial mental outlet for me, clearing my head and providing a valuable "daily reset" that helps maintain my overall well-being and focus.

Technical Growth

All my free time around the house is dedicated to expanding my coding knowledge and skills. I have a deep interest in understanding how things work at a fundamental level, which drives my passion for technology and programming. This curiosity keeps me constantly learning and exploring new concepts.

Structured Discipline

I believe in maintaining a highly structured schedule to maximize productivity. My approach involves tracking progress at multiple levels: daily tasks feed into weekly goals, which support monthly objectives, creating a clear path toward my long-term vision. This systematic planning helps me stay focused and accountable.

Future Vision

My structured planning extends up to 6 months ahead, with everything building toward my ultimate goal of launching my own app/web development company after completing university. This clear direction gives purpose to my intense work ethic and continuous self-improvement.
